The End of Team Etile
On 3/19/2017, Team Etile was discontinued.
Team Etile, if you couldn't tell already, was a joke
"hacker" group with a few members in it. There was a
levelling system, a way we would "hack" people called
taping, and, well, a lot of edgy jokes. The main reason
I made this site was to add to the early 2000 dumb edgy
style of this, and also to test out my knowledge of HTML.
Not all of the pages were finished. Instead, Chen, Rocket
and Sovn were the only pages for our members created.
I had more little plans for this site, and well, Team
Etile was already getting kinda stale with the same jokes.
Eventually, I left Members Arena, BTS, and a lot of other servers
and then I posted a message in Team Etile saying how I was
thinking of quitting Team Etile. Rocket agreed, and the reason
I wanted to was because I basically lost my reputation with
the Builders community and nobody in MA liked me. This
ended up making everyone leave the server and me deleting it.
The sad part about this was that now I've kind of lost contact
with the only people I liked in the community. I would rejoin
BTS, but I just don't wanna be associated with it and only talk
to the friends I actually wanna keep. So, this page is no longer
being updated and I'll be focusing on my main webpage.
